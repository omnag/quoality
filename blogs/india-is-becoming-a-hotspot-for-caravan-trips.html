India is rapidly rising as a worldwide traveler destination, way to its wonderful topography. From pristine seashores to sand dunes and powerful mountains to dense forests, the nation has it all. And with regards to adapting to new tour trends, India isn’t a long way behind.

As of now, the country is experiencing the idea of caravan tourism, which has received a significant reputational around international due to the liberty and versatility it offers. Now, with the COVID-19 scare still lingering, it’s also one of the more secure approaches to tour.

In fact, the Ministry of Tourism in India, of their New Tourism Products Annual Report 2009–10, formally recognized the way of life of Caravan Tourism withinside the country. Recently, Madhya Pradesh tourism too shared photos of its luxurious caravans on social media, inviting travelers to discover the state without compromising on social distancing norms.

What is a Caravan?

A caravan is a motor car or van that has a bed and cooking gadget and is used to tread across offbeat routes and places. It may be utilized by a group making plans for an adventurous journey. The vehicle is such that it may be effortlessly pulled with the help of a car. Camping amid nature and exploring all of the offbeat trails, will simply broaden your traveling experience.

So, in case you are a person who’s bored of the monotonous manner of traveling, a caravan journey is an exceptional shot for you! India too is getting on board with this vintage tour style and now that social distancing has come to be a necessity, caravan trips are destined to be the thing. Though India has a protracted manner to move in phrases of caravan-friendly spots, there are a few cool locations in which you can park your caravans and relax!

Here are a few spots where you can go and visit with your caravans!

Gokharna

Gokarna in Karnataka is one of those beach destinations where people relax surrounded by nature. The beaches here are perfect for those seeking solitude and tranquility. There is plenty of space to park your caravan and experience those moments.

Rishikesh

Located on the banks of the Ganges, Rishikesh is a place where spirituality abounds in every nook and cranny. This beautiful destination is known for its campsites, and with permission, you can easily park your car and enjoy the peace and quiet.

Coorg

Coorg is one of the most scenic destinations in South India. However, with its winding roads and winding turns, one must be a safe and skilled driver. Once there, you can park your vehicle at one of the campsites and enjoy the stunning scenery.

Hampi

Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is visited by several travelers each year. There are several campsites and homestays where you can park your caravan and enjoy the destination’s golden history.

Sakleshpur

Only 5 to 6 hours drive from Bengaluru is Sakleshpur, a well-known campsite in the south. One can travel from Bengaluru to Sakleshpur with his caravan and engage in countless interesting activities including trekking.

Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, the golden city of Rajasthan, is famous for its desert camps and sand dunes. This is the best place to arrive with your caravan and explore the beauty, culture and cuisine of Rajasthan.
