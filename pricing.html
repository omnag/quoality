Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. Once you've had the demo session with us, you can have a free trial of our product.
Do I need to add a credit card to register for the free trial?
No. Not at all! The free trial is yours to use without having to add any payment details.
Is there a contract?
Yes, there is a contract. You can either opt for a monthly based contract or a yearly based contract with a discount on top of it.
Can I switch from a month to month to a yearly contract?
Yes. You can initially use our monthly based contract to get used to our product and then quite conveniently switch to a yearly contract after you're all settled with the product's working
Are there any additional fees/commissions that I should be aware of?
No. Quoality doesn't charge any additional fess or commissions. If you wish to transfer your data from your previous existing systems, our team will do that for you for a one time fee.
When will I receive a receipt?
After every transaction you make, you will receive a receipt on your company’s registered email address.

Can’t find the answer you’re looking for? Please chat to our friendly team.
